Natus Vincere (Latin for "born to win", often abbreviated to NAVI and previously Na`Vi) is a Ukrainian professional esports organization.
They were the first team to win all Counter-Strike Majors in one calendar year, with Intel Extreme Masters IV, ESWC 2010, and WCG 2010, and the first team to win a Global Offensive Major without dropping a map, doing so at PGL Major Stockholm 2021.

NAVI also made history as the first team to win a Counter-Strike 2 Major, claiming victory at PGL Major Copenhagen 2024.

Records

[edit]
  • Hold the record for most Counter-Strike majors won in a row with 4 titles.
  • They are the only organization to have participated in all 23 CS:GO and CS2 Majors, making the playoffs a record 18 times, ahead of Fnatic with 13 playoff appearances.
  • They also have the record for most Major final appearances at 6.
  • Most Major finals lost at 4.
    • Tied with Ninjas in Pyjamas and FaZe.
  • First team to win a CS:GO Major without dropping a map, doing so at PGL Major Stockholm 2021.
  • Third team to win an Intel Grand Slam after Astralis and Team Liquid.
  • Has the third-longest map win streak in CS:GO and CS2 Big Events history with 19 consecutive map victories. [81]
